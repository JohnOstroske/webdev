Exploring Shipwrecks

The Great Lakes can be very dangerous for ships and boats, and Lake Michigan has a huge number of ships wrecks. The Mbes Pe'matse'juk may not be aware of as many of these wrecks as those on land. After all, those on land were aware of the ships existance and destination. The Mbes Pe'matse'juk do enjoy searching for, and exploring wrecks of yesteryear.

Theatre and Dance

The Mbes Pe'matse'juk are excellent in the field of Theatre. Artistic, creative, and not as restricted by gravity as their land dwelling counterparts, the Mbes Pe'matse'juk have created dance numbers and stage effects of unsurpassed awe and beauty.
