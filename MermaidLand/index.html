Welcome to the city of:
Mbes Pe'Metse'Juk Kwetak
The Water People Hill

This undersea Lake Michigan Village is located in the middle of Lake Michigan, just north of Grand Rapids and Milwaukee. This village is almost 800 years old, and the residence are humans with fish tails adept at life under the sea. The Mbes Pe'Metse'Juk were known as traders, partners, and friends with the Pottawatomi, but largely went into hiding when the Pottawatomi tribes were driven out.

Come and explore the history and culture of the fascinating Mbes Pe'Metse'Juk tribe.
